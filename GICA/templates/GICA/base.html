About us

Gonder Alemaqef Islamic Association, abbreviated as GAIA, has set itself the task of protecting, repairing, motivating, educating, caring for and helping to build people in difficulty. The values and the principles of action of the association find their concretization in the associative project and the principles of accompaniment of the fragile public.
